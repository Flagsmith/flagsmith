### Is Flagsmith SOC 2 certified?

Yes, Flagsmith is SOC 2 Type 2 certified.

### How do I delete an Organisation?
Go to **Organisation** > **Manage**. Scroll to the very bottom, click the trash can icon next to "Delete Organisation," and confirm the name. This is irreversible.

<div className="admonition admonition-warning alert alert--warning">
  <div className="admonition-heading">
    <h5>Warning</h5>
  </div>
  <div className="admonition-content">
    <p>This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All projects, flags, and data within the organisation will be deleted.</p>

</div>

### How do I downgrade to the Free Plan from a paid plan?
Go to **Organisation** > **Manage** > **Manage Invoices**. This opens the billing portal where you can modify your subscription.

**Related:** [How do I update my payment details?](#how-do-i-update-my-payment-details)

### How do I delete an account?
Go to **Organisation** > **Manage** > **Team Members**, and click the trash can icon next to the user account you wish to remove.

**Related:** [How do I add a new person to my organisation?](#how-do-i-add-a-new-person-to-my-organisation)

### Does Flagsmith have G Suite or does Flagsmith Support SSO?
Yes. Google and GitHub SSO are supported on **all plans** (including Free). SAML/Okta/AD is supported on Scale-Up and Enterprise plans.

**Related:** [Can the open source version be configured to connect to an LDAP server?](/support/faq/open-source-self-hosted#can-the-open-source-version-be-configured-to-connect-to-an-ldap-server)

**Related documentation:** [OAuth](/administration-and-security/access-control/oauth) • [SAML](/administration-and-security/access-control/saml) • [Okta](/administration-and-security/access-control/okta)

### How can I change the email associated with my account?
You cannot do this yourself in the UI. You must contact Flagsmith support.

**Related:** [Contact Support](/support)

### How do I update my payment details?
Go to **Organisation** > **Manage** > **Manage Invoices** to access the billing portal.

**Related:** [How do I view past invoices?](#how-do-i-view-past-invoices)

### How do I add a new person to my organisation?
Go to **Organisation** > **Edit** > **Team Members**. You can either **Invite Members** by email or generate an **Invite Link** to share.

**Related documentation:** [RBAC](/administration-and-security/access-control/rbac)

### How do I view past invoices?
Go to **Organisation** > **Edit** > **Manage Invoices**.

**Related:** [How do I update my payment details?](#how-do-i-update-my-payment-details)
